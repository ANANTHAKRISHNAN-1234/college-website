ABOUT US

The College of Engineering Chengannur (CEC), was
established by the Government of Kerala under the aegis of
the IHRD, in the year 1993. It has been approved by All India
Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and affiliated to APJ
Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU). Ever since its
inception, the college has maintained its eminence as a
leading engineering college in Kerala. Being located in
Chengannur town, Alleppey district the college has got
access to all means of transport, communication and
lodging facilities.
